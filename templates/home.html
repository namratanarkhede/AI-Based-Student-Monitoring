Welcome !!!!

Our AI-based student monitoring system helps schools and universities keep track of student performance and behavior. By analyzing data from various sources such as attendance records, test scores, and even social media activity, our system can identify at-risk students and provide early interventions to help them succeed.


With our system, schools can also monitor for incidents such as cyberbullying and potential security threats. Our platform is customizable to fit the unique needs of each institution and can be integrated with existing systems and tools.
