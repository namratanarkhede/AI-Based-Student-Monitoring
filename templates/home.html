Welcome !!!!

Our AI-based student monitoring system helps schools and universities keep track of student performance and behavior. Our AI-based student monitoring system is designed to simplify the task of monitoring student attendance and provide valuable insights to school administrators. With the help of face recognition technology, our system automatically records student attendance, making it easier for teachers to focus on their teaching instead of manual attendance taking. The system also calculates the attendance percentage of each student and generates subject-wise attendance reports for further analysis.

The visualization module provides graphical representation of attendance data, making it easier for administrators to identify students who have good or poor attendance records. The system sends alerts to students and their parents via email when their attendance falls below a certain threshold. This helps to ensure that students are aware of their attendance records and take corrective actions to improve their attendance. Our system is an effective tool for both teachers and students, helping to improve academic performance and promote a culture of attendance and accountability in organizations and schools.
